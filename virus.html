VIRUS

Understanding Viruses

Viruses are microscopic organisms that can infect humans, animals, and plants. They spread in different ways and may cause diseases ranging from the common cold to more serious illnesses.

What Is a Virus?

A virus is a tiny infectious agent that can only reproduce inside the cells of a living host. Viruses contain genetic material surrounded by a protective coat.

How Viruses Spread

  • Airborne droplets from coughing or sneezing
  • Direct physical contact
  • Contaminated food or water
  • Insect bites
  • Contact with infected surfaces

Prevention Tips

  • Wash your hands regularly
  • Cover your mouth when coughing or sneezing
  • Keep vaccinations up to date
  • Maintain good hygiene practices
  • Avoid close contact with sick individuals
